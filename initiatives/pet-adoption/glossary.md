# Pet Adoption Glossary

## Pet
An animal registered in the system that is available (or was previously available) for adoption.

## Listing
A public-facing entry for a Pet, including its profile details, photos, and availability status.

## Adopter
A registered user who has submitted at least one Adoption Application.

## Adoption Application
A formal request submitted by a registered user to adopt a specific Pet. Contains the applicant's details,
home environment information, and any prior pet ownership history.

## Shelter
An organization that registers Pets and manages Listings on the platform.
Also known as Rescue Center or Animal Welfare Organization.

## PetMatch
The external matching service used to recommend Pets to potential Adopters based on lifestyle compatibility.
